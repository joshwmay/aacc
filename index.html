Anne Arundel Community College Assessment

Located in the heart of the DMV, AACC is a great option for those seeking higher education. The school recently added the Clauson Center for Innovation and Skilled Trades thanks to a sizable donation and a notable fundraising campaign. With the second highest percentage of GI Bill recipients and fifth highest percentage students over the age of 25 amongst Maryland Community Colleges, Anne Arundel Community College is a great continued education option for recently separated veterans and working adults; as well as young adults transitioning out of high school. The proximity to the State Capital, Fort Meade, Baltimore, the District of Columbia, and an array of federal agencies pit the school in the middle of a caldera of job and internship opportunities.
