Maryland Community Colleges Dashboard

Highlighting Anne Arundel Community College

This dashboard was prepared for the purpose of better understanding how Anne Arundel Community College compares to other community colleges in Maryland. It approaches the task in a tangible way fit for mobile devices. Each visual is interactive and opens up further details when a datapoint is interacted with. This webpage serves prospective students and educators in an application setting that thoughtfully considers all Maryland community colleges with a bevy supporting information and links to each institution.Below is a 5-panel dashboard answering questions about topics like tuition pricing, instructor pay, student veteran populations, and vaccination requirements.

Anne Arundel
Community College
Assessment

Located in the heart of the DMV, AACC is a great option for those seeking higher education. The school recently added the Clauson Center for Innovation and Skilled Trades thanks to a sizable donation and a notable fundraising campaign. With the second highest percentage of GI Bill recipients and fifth highest percentage students over the age of 25 amongst Maryland Community Colleges, Anne Arundel Community College is a great continued education option for recently separated veterans and working adults.

AACC welcomes a large diverse student body, opening up potential for networking amongst peers and finding student groups covering a wider variety of interests. Additionally, the geography and proximity to the State Capital, Fort Meade, Baltimore, the District of Columbia, and an array of federal agencies perch the school on the ridge of a job and internship opportunity caldera.
