Assessing Anne Arundel Community College

Located in the heart of the DMV, AACC is a great option for those seeking higher education. The interactive visuals above illustrate the value of an education at AACC by familiarizing users with things like tuition pricing, professor salaries, and a mapped out glimpse into all Maryland community colleges. These visuals can help prospective students or educators decide if AACC is right for them, or even assist in more specific organizational/analysis matters like assessing pay scales.

Emphasizing the 'Community' in 'Community College', the school recently expanded thanks to a sizable donation and a notable fundraising campaign. With a sizable percentage of both GI Bill recipients and students over the age of 25, Anne Arundel Community College is a great continued education option for recently separated veterans and working adults. The proximity to the State Capital, Fort Meade, Baltimore, the District of Columbia, and several federal facilities pit the school in the middle of a caldera of job and internship opportunities.
